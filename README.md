# Finance, Quantitative Research, and Valuation Projects
This repository contains my self-directed projects in quantitative finance, financial modeling, market analysis, macroeconomic research, and sentiment-based trading. The goal of the repository is to document my development in finance, programming, and empirical market research through practical projects built mainly in Python/Jupyter Notebook and Excel.

My work is organized into three main areas:

- **Quantitative finance and market research** — notebooks on discrete-time finance, continuous-time finance, value-at-risk, portfolio analysis, macroeconomic analysis, stock-market patterns, and sentiment-based strategies.
- **Financial modeling and valuation** — Excel-based company models and sector models covering public companies across technology, software, data centers, crypto-related infrastructure, consumer, healthcare, and other sectors.
- **Mini projects** — smaller Excel exercises and finance-modeling examples, including option pricing, Greeks, Monte Carlo simulation, barrier options, probability of default, and historical simulation VaR.
