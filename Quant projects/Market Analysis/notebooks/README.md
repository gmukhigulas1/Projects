# Notebooks

This folder contains exploratory notebooks related to stock-market analysis, price patterns, simple strategy testing, and portfolio-style experiments.

The notebooks here are smaller and more experimental than the sentiment trading research project. I use them to test ideas quickly and practice working with market data in Python.

## Main themes

The notebooks cover topics such as:

- stock-data exploration;
- Markowitz-style or portfolio-related experiments;
- moving-average or trend-based strategy ideas.
